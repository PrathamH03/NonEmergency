Welcome to Community Resolve

Your platform for reporting non-emergency issues in your community. From potholes to graffiti, our system makes reporting simple and ensures your concerns are addressed promptly.

Some problems belong to non-emergency!

Waste Collection

The absence of dustbins results in litter accumulation in public spaces, affecting cleanliness.

Street Lighting

Poor street lighting in certain areas leads to safety issues during nighttime.

Graffiti

Graffiti on public walls can negatively impact the appearance of the community.

Potholes

Potholes on roads pose a danger to both pedestrians and vehicles, causing accidents.

Manhole Covers

Broken or missing manhole covers create dangerous situations for pedestrians.

Water Pipeline

Leaking or broken water pipelines lead to water wastage and public inconvenience.
